1. Overview

HenWorks ("we", "us", or "our") is an independent software developer based in Taiwan. We build digital tools and applications with a focus on user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information across our website (henworks.com) and our products.

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our services, and we never sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Products Covered by This Policy

TextLen

Mac App · OCR

Local AI-powered OCR. All processing happens on your device. No data leaves your Mac.

Opclaw

Android TV · Productivity

Uses account-based cloud sync. Requires sign-in. Firebase services are used for authentication and data storage.

Each product may also have its own dedicated Privacy Policy page with more detail.

3. This Website (henworks.com)

Our website does not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics platform. We do not track individual visitors or use fingerprinting technologies.

Standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamp) may be retained by our hosting provider (Cloudflare) for security and performance purposes. These logs are not used to identify individual users and are subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

henworks.com uses only essential functional cookies (e.g., language preference). No advertising or tracking cookies are set on this website.

4. TextLen (Mac App)

TextLen does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All OCR processing is performed entirely on your device using on-device AI models. Images, documents, and recognized text never leave your Mac.

TextLen does not include crash reporting, analytics SDKs, or telemetry of any kind. No network requests are made unless you explicitly use an online feature (such as downloading an AI model update).

Model downloads are served over HTTPS from our CDN. Only basic download metadata (file name, file size) is transmitted during this process.

In-App Advertising

TextLen may display non-personalized advertisements via an in-app WebView. These ads are served from henworks.com and may include Google AdSense units. When AdSense is active, Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

For more details, see the TextLen Privacy Policy.

5. Opclaw (Android TV App)

Opclaw requires user account registration to provide cloud sync features. We collect:

  • Account information: Email address, used for authentication via Firebase Auth.
  • App usage data: Feature usage statistics collected via Firebase Analytics to improve the app.
  • User-generated content: Data you create or save within Opclaw, stored in Firebase Firestore and associated with your account.

Firebase services are operated by Google LLC. Data may be stored on servers in the United States and other countries. For details, see Firebase Privacy and Security.

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@henworks.com.

For more details, see the Opclaw Privacy Policy.
