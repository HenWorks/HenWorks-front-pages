Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

At Hen Works, your privacy is our top priority. TextLen processes all OCR entirely on your Mac — your images and results never leave your device.

🔒
100% On-Device Processing

All OCR processing runs locally on your Mac's Neural Engine. Your images, documents, and OCR results are never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

Information We Collect

Authentication

  • Email Address: Collected during account creation via Firebase Auth (Google) for secure login and verification.

Usage Data

  • Usage Count: Number of OCR operations performed, used to manage subscription tiers.
  • Purchase History: Records of in-app purchases and subscriptions via Apple StoreKit.

Images & OCR Results

🚫
Never Uploaded Your images are processed 100% on-device and never sent to any server.
🗑️
Never Stored OCR results stay in your local app memory and are never persisted externally.

Third-Party Services

TextLen integrates with the following trusted services:

  • Firebase Auth (Google): Handles secure authentication and email verification.
  • Apple StoreKit: Manages in-app purchases and subscription billing through Apple's secure infrastructure.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Processes subscription events and minimal usage analytics.

Data Storage & Location

Limited user data (email, usage count, purchase history) is stored in Firebase databases located in asia-east1 (Taiwan) for optimal performance and compliance with local regulations.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

📋
Access Request a copy of your personal data at any time.
🗑️
Delete Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
📦
Export Download your data in a portable format.
🔕
Opt-Out Disable analytics and non-essential data collection.

To exercise these rights, contact info@henworks.com

In-App Advertising

TextLen may display advertisements within the app via an embedded web view. Ads are served from henworks.com and may include:

  • Self-hosted ads: Promotions for HenWorks products and services.
  • Google AdSense: Third-party advertisements served by Google. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to websites.
  • Affiliate links: Recommended products or services. We may receive a commission if you make a purchase through these links. All affiliate content is clearly labeled.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Children's Privacy

TextLen is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Contact Us

  • Website: https://www.henworks.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of TextLen following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

隱私政策

最後更新：2026年3月26日

在 Hen Works，您的隱私是我們的首要任務。TextLen 的所有 OCR 處理完全在您的 Mac 上進行 — 您的影像和結果永遠不會離開您的裝置。

🔒
100% 本機端處理

所有 OCR 處理都在您 Mac 的 Neural Engine 上本地執行。您的影像、文件和 OCR 結果絕不會上傳到任何伺服器或與第三方共享。

我們收集的資訊

身份驗證

  • 電子郵件地址：透過 Firebase Auth (Google) 在帳戶建立期間收集，用於安全登入和驗證。

使用資料

  • 使用次數：執行的 OCR 操作次數，用於管理訂閱層級。
  • 購買歷史：透過 Apple StoreKit 進行的應用內購買和訂閱記錄。

影像與 OCR 結果

🚫
永不上傳 您的影像 100% 在裝置上處理，絕不發送至任何伺服器。
🗑️
永不儲存 OCR 結果保留在您的本機應用記憶體中，絕不會被外部保留。

第三方服務

TextLen 整合以下受信任的服務：

  • Firebase Auth (Google)：處理安全身份驗證和電子郵件驗證。
  • Apple StoreKit：透過 Apple 的安全基礎設施管理應用內購買和訂閱計費。
  • Firebase Cloud Functions：處理訂閱事件和最小限度的使用分析。

資料儲存與位置

有限的用戶資料（電子郵件、使用次數、購買歷史）儲存在位於 asia-east1（台灣）的 Firebase 資料庫中，以實現最佳效能和遵守當地法規。

プライバシーポリシー

最終更新：2026年3月26日

Hen Worksでは、お客様のプライバシーを最優先にしています。TextLenのすべてのOCR処理はお客様のMac上で完結します — 画像や結果がデバイスを離れることはありません。

🔒
100% オンデバイス処理

すべてのOCR処理はお客様のMacのNeural Engine上でローカルに実行されます。画像、ドキュメント、OCR結果は一切サーバーにアップロードされず、第三者と共有されることもありません。

当社が収集する情報

認証

  • メールアドレス：Firebase Auth（Google）を介したアカウント作成時に収集。安全なログインと認証に使用。

使用データ

  • 使用回数：実行されたOCR操作の数。サブスクリプション管理に使用。
  • 購入履歴：Apple StoreKitを通じたアプリ内購入とサブスクリプションの記録。

画像とOCR結果

🚫
アップロードされません お客様の画像は100%デバイス上で処理され、サーバーに送信されることはありません。
🗑️
保存されません OCR結果はローカルアプリのメモリに保持され、外部に永続化されることはありません。

第三者サービス

TextLenは以下の信頼できるサービスと統合されています：

  • Firebase Auth（Google）：安全な認証とメール検証を処理。
  • Apple StoreKit：Appleの安全なインフラを通じてアプリ内購入とサブスクリプション請求を管理。
  • Firebase Cloud Functions：サブスクリプションイベントと最小限の使用分析を処理。

データストレージと場所

限定的なユーザーデータ（メール、使用回数、購入履歴）は、最適なパフォーマンスと現地法規遵守のためにasia-east1（台湾）に位置するFirebaseデータベースに保存されます。

Hen Works에서 귀하의 개인정보는 최우선 과제입니다. TextLen의 모든 OCR 처리는 귀하의 Mac에서 완전히 이루어집니다 — 이미지와 결과는 절대 기기를 떠나지 않습니다.

🔒
100% 온디바이스 처리

모든 OCR 처리는 귀하의 Mac Neural Engine에서 로컬로 실행됩니다. 이미지, 문서, OCR 결과는 어떤 서버에도 업로드되지 않으며 제3자와 공유되지 않습니다.

당사가 수집하는 정보

인증

  • 이메일 주소: Firebase Auth(Google)를 통한 계정 생성 시 수집. 안전한 로그인 및 인증에 사용.

사용 데이터

  • 사용 횟수: 실행된 OCR 작업 횟수. 구독 관리에 사용.
  • 구매 이력: Apple StoreKit을 통한 앱 내 구매 및 구독 기록.

이미지 및 OCR 결과

🚫
업로드되지 않음 귀하의 이미지는 100% 기기에서 처리되며 서버로 전송되지 않습니다.
🗑️
저장되지 않음 OCR 결과는 로컬 앱 메모리에 유지되며 외부에 영구 저장되지 않습니다.

제3자 서비스

TextLen은 다음과 같은 신뢰할 수 있는 서비스와 통합됩니다:

  • Firebase Auth(Google): 안전한 인증 및 이메일 인증 처리.
  • Apple StoreKit: Apple의 안전한 인프라를 통해 앱 내 구매 및 구독 청구 관리.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: 구독 이벤트 및 최소한의 사용 분석 처리.

데이터 저장 및 위치

제한된 사용자 데이터(이메일, 사용 횟수, 구매 이력)는 최적의 성능 및 현지 규정 준수를 위해 asia-east1(대만)에 위치한 Firebase 데이터베이스에 저장됩니다.
